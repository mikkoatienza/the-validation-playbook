## Stage Rules

<Callout type="warn" title="Keep it honest">
- Commitment beats compliments. Time or money is the signal.
- Price is a test, not a guess.
- Channels must reach buyers, not just traffic.
- If the commitment is weak, fix the offer before you build.
</Callout>

Speed matters here, but evidence matters more. Earn the signal.

- Treating "likes" as demand
- Discounting before testing real willingness to pay
- Blaming the channel instead of fixing the offer
- Pitching to friends instead of buyers
- Chasing every channel at once
</Callout>
