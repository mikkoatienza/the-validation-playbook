## What a Channel Is

A channel is where your buyers already are and already paying attention. It is not a tactic. It is a path to access. An audience might watch. A channel lets you reach a buyer and ask for a next step.

<Callout type="warn" title="The 3-channel rule">
Pick only two or three channels to test. More than that is noise. You are not scaling. You are probing. Small tests beat big plans. Two or three is enough to learn in one week.
</Callout>

<Callout type="error" title="Common traps">
- Chasing every channel at once
- Counting clicks instead of conversations
- Mistaking attention for buyer access
- Changing the message mid-test
- Assuming volume will fix a weak message
</Callout>
