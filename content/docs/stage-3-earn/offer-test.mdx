Your offer is the first real proof that your idea deserves to exist. A feature list can get compliments. An offer asks for commitment. Only one of those predicts demand.

## What an Offer Is

An offer is a clear promise to a specific person. It is not a list of features. It is a commitment exchange.

    <p className="mt-2 text-[10px] italic" style={{color: 'hsl(var(--stage-3))'}}>Describes what the customer gets</p>
  </div>

If you cannot say the outcome in one line, you are not ready to ask for commitment.

Use plain language, not buzzwords. Lead with the outcome, not the tech. If a buyer has to decode your sentence, they will not commit to it.
