This module answers a brutal question: is the problem real enough to build around? A problem can sound smart, but if it does not cost people time, money, or risk, it will not survive.

<Callout type="idea" title="Output for this module">
- Problem scorecard for top 3 candidates
- Evidence notes for each score
- One prioritized problem to take into Module 3
</Callout>

## The Problem Scorecard

Score each problem on four factors (1-5). The highest combined score moves forward.

<div className="mt-4 grid gap-3 sm:grid-cols-2">
  <div className="rounded-xl border-2 border-fd-border p-4 transition-all hover:border-[hsl(var(--stage-2)/0.3)]">
    <div className="flex items-center gap-2"><span className="flex h-7 w-7 items-center justify-center rounded-lg text-xs font-bold text-white" style={{backgroundColor: 'hsl(var(--stage-2))'}}>F</span><span className="text-sm font-semibold text-fd-foreground">Frequency</span></div>
    <p className="mt-1.5 text-xs text-fd-muted-foreground">How often does the pain show up?</p>
    <div className="mt-2 space-y-0.5 text-[10px] text-fd-muted-foreground"><p>1 = rare (a few times a year)</p><p>3 = monthly</p><p className="font-semibold" style={{color: 'hsl(var(--stage-2))'}}>5 = weekly or daily</p></div>
  </div>
  <div className="rounded-xl border-2 border-fd-border p-4 transition-all hover:border-[hsl(var(--stage-2)/0.3)]">
    <div className="flex items-center gap-2"><span className="flex h-7 w-7 items-center justify-center rounded-lg text-xs font-bold text-white" style={{backgroundColor: 'hsl(var(--stage-2))'}}>I</span><span className="text-sm font-semibold text-fd-foreground">Intensity</span></div>
    <p className="mt-1.5 text-xs text-fd-muted-foreground">How costly is it when it hits?</p>
    <div className="mt-2 space-y-0.5 text-[10px] text-fd-muted-foreground"><p>1 = mild annoyance</p><p>3 = real friction, tolerable</p><p className="font-semibold" style={{color: 'hsl(var(--stage-2))'}}>5 = real cost, risk, or lost revenue</p></div>
  </div>
  <div className="rounded-xl border-2 border-fd-border p-4 transition-all hover:border-[hsl(var(--stage-2)/0.3)]">
    <div className="flex items-center gap-2"><span className="flex h-7 w-7 items-center justify-center rounded-lg text-xs font-bold text-white" style={{backgroundColor: 'hsl(var(--stage-2))'}}>S</span><span className="text-sm font-semibold text-fd-foreground">Spend</span></div>
    <p className="mt-1.5 text-xs text-fd-muted-foreground">Is there evidence of time or money spent?</p>
    <div className="mt-2 space-y-0.5 text-[10px] text-fd-muted-foreground"><p>1 = no spend, no time invested</p><p>3 = time investment or small tools</p><p className="font-semibold" style={{color: 'hsl(var(--stage-2))'}}>5 = clear spend or paid solutions</p></div>
  </div>
  <div className="rounded-xl border-2 border-fd-border p-4 transition-all hover:border-[hsl(var(--stage-2)/0.3)]">
    <div className="flex items-center gap-2"><span className="flex h-7 w-7 items-center justify-center rounded-lg text-xs font-bold text-white" style={{backgroundColor: 'hsl(var(--stage-2))'}}>W</span><span className="text-sm font-semibold text-fd-foreground">Workarounds</span></div>
    <p className="mt-1.5 text-xs text-fd-muted-foreground">Are people actively patching it today?</p>
    <div className="mt-2 space-y-0.5 text-[10px] text-fd-muted-foreground"><p>1 = nothing in place</p><p>3 = light hacks or manual steps</p><p className="font-semibold" style={{color: 'hsl(var(--stage-2))'}}>5 = repeatable, visible workarounds</p></div>
  </div>
</div>

If you are not sure, score lower. Overconfidence hides weak pain. Raden built smart luggage with tech upgrades -- sold out early, shut down in 2018. The pain was not deep enough. Away scaled by focusing on the core pain of travel. Nice-to-have collapsed. Painful alternative took off.

## Evidence Prompts

Use these questions to collect evidence for each factor score.

<div className="mt-4 grid gap-3 sm:grid-cols-2">
  <div className="rounded-xl border border-fd-border p-4"><p className="text-xs font-semibold" style={{color: 'hsl(var(--stage-2))'}}>Frequency</p><p className="mt-1 text-xs text-fd-muted-foreground">"When was the last time this happened?" -- "How often does it show up in a normal week?"</p></div>
  <div className="rounded-xl border border-fd-border p-4"><p className="text-xs font-semibold" style={{color: 'hsl(var(--stage-2))'}}>Intensity</p><p className="mt-1 text-xs text-fd-muted-foreground">"What breaks when it happens?" -- "What does it cost you when it goes wrong?"</p></div>
  <div className="rounded-xl border border-fd-border p-4"><p className="text-xs font-semibold" style={{color: 'hsl(var(--stage-2))'}}>Spend</p><p className="mt-1 text-xs text-fd-muted-foreground">"Have you paid for anything to solve it?" -- "What tools do you already use?"</p></div>
  <div className="rounded-xl border border-fd-border p-4"><p className="text-xs font-semibold" style={{color: 'hsl(var(--stage-2))'}}>Workarounds</p><p className="mt-1 text-xs text-fd-muted-foreground">"What do you do instead today?" -- "Can you show me how you handle it?"</p></div>
</div>

No evidence = no points. Scores without evidence are just guesses.

## Root Cause Check
