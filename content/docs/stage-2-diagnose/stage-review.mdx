## What Comes Next

You are about to move from "Is this problem worth solving?" to "Will anyone commit to a solution?" Stage 3 will test whether people will commit to your offer and at what price. That work only makes sense if the Stage 2 diagnosis is honest.

Resist the urge to decorate the offer before you test it. Stage 3 is about commitment, not persuasion.
