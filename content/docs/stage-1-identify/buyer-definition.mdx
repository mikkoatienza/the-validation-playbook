<div className="mx-auto max-w-3xl">

You can have a real pain and a clear solution and still fail if the wrong person controls the budget. Users can love you, but buyers usually decide.

This module makes the decision path explicit. You will define the Ideal Customer Profile (ICP), separate user from buyer, and map who triggers, approves, and pays.

<Callout type="idea" title="Output for this module">
- 1 to 2 ICP profiles
- Buyer/user split with decision context
- A clear access path to the buyer
</Callout>

## Buyer vs User

<div className="mt-4 grid gap-4 sm:grid-cols-2">
  <div className="rounded-xl border-2 border-fd-border p-5" style={{borderLeftWidth: '3px', borderLeftColor: 'hsl(var(--stage-1))'}}>
    <p className="text-sm font-semibold text-fd-foreground">User</p>
    <p className="mt-1 text-xs text-fd-muted-foreground">Feels the pain. Presses the buttons. Wants ease.</p>
    <div className="mt-3 space-y-1.5 text-xs text-fd-muted-foreground">
      <p>Support agent needs a faster ticket tool</p>
      <p>Designer wants a better asset library</p>
      <p>Teacher wants a better classroom tool</p>
    </div>
    <p className="mt-3 text-[10px] italic text-fd-muted-foreground">Can be loud. Cannot write checks.</p>
  </div>
  <div className="rounded-xl border-2 border-fd-border p-5" style={{borderLeftWidth: '3px', borderLeftColor: 'hsl(var(--stage-1))'}}>
    <p className="text-sm font-semibold text-fd-foreground">Buyer</p>
    <p className="mt-1 text-xs text-fd-muted-foreground">Funds the fix. Controls the budget. Cares about outcomes and risk.</p>
    <div className="mt-3 space-y-1.5 text-xs text-fd-muted-foreground">
      <p>Support manager who owns the budget</p>
      <p>Head of marketing who approves software</p>
      <p>School or district that controls purchasing</p>
    </div>
    <p className="mt-3 text-[10px] italic" style={{color: 'hsl(var(--stage-1))'}}>Writes checks. Makes decisions.</p>
  </div>
</div>

Sometimes they are the same person. Often they are not. If you cannot name the buyer, you are still guessing. Teachmint learned this when they built for teachers but discovered budgets lived with schools and districts. Wrong audience, weak sales, then a buyer shift.

## Buyer Signals

You are not looking for enthusiasm. You are looking for evidence of decision power.

<div className="mt-4 grid gap-3 sm:grid-cols-2">
  <div className="rounded-xl border-2 border-fd-border p-4 transition-all hover:border-[hsl(var(--stage-1)/0.3)]">
    <div className="flex items-center gap-2">
      <span className="flex h-7 w-7 items-center justify-center rounded-lg text-xs font-bold text-white" style={{backgroundColor: 'hsl(var(--stage-1))'}}>$</span>
      <span className="text-sm font-semibold text-fd-foreground">Budget control</span>
    </div>
    <p className="mt-1.5 text-xs text-fd-muted-foreground">They own a line item, expense tools without approval, or can approve a pilot.</p>
  </div>
  <div className="rounded-xl border-2 border-fd-border p-4 transition-all hover:border-[hsl(var(--stage-1)/0.3)]">
    <div className="flex items-center gap-2">
      <span className="flex h-7 w-7 items-center justify-center rounded-lg text-xs font-bold text-white" style={{backgroundColor: 'hsl(var(--stage-1))'}}>K</span>
      <span className="text-sm font-semibold text-fd-foreground">KPI ownership</span>
    </div>
    <p className="mt-1.5 text-xs text-fd-muted-foreground">They have a metric tied to the pain -- retention, revenue, compliance, speed.</p>
  </div>
  <div className="rounded-xl border-2 border-fd-border p-4 transition-all hover:border-[hsl(var(--stage-1)/0.3)]">
    <div className="flex items-center gap-2">
      <span className="flex h-7 w-7 items-center justify-center rounded-lg text-xs font-bold text-white" style={{backgroundColor: 'hsl(var(--stage-1))'}}>P</span>
      <span className="text-sm font-semibold text-fd-foreground">Prior purchase</span>
    </div>
    <p className="mt-1.5 text-xs text-fd-muted-foreground">They have bought similar tools in the last 12 months.</p>
  </div>
  <div className="rounded-xl border-2 border-fd-border p-4 transition-all hover:border-[hsl(var(--stage-1)/0.3)]">
    <div className="flex items-center gap-2">
      <span className="flex h-7 w-7 items-center justify-center rounded-lg text-xs font-bold text-white" style={{backgroundColor: 'hsl(var(--stage-1))'}}>A</span>
      <span className="text-sm font-semibold text-fd-foreground">Approval authority</span>
    </div>
    <p className="mt-1.5 text-xs text-fd-muted-foreground">They can say yes without checking with someone else.</p>
  </div>
</div>

If none of those are true, you are still talking to a user. Unknown is better than wrong -- this is where many founders lose months.

## Map the Buyer Path

Most purchases follow a path. Your job is to map it early.

## Buyer Thesis

Write one sentence that names the buyer and the outcome they pay for.

<div className="mt-4 rounded-xl border-2 border-fd-border p-5" style={{backgroundColor: 'hsl(var(--fd-muted) / 0.4)'}}>
  <p className="text-[10px] font-semibold uppercase tracking-wider" style={{color: 'hsl(var(--stage-1))'}}>Buyer Thesis Formula</p>
  <p className="mt-3 font-[family-name:var(--font-display)] text-xl text-fd-foreground">"The buyer is <strong>[role]</strong> who will pay to <strong>[outcome]</strong> because <strong>[cost of pain]</strong>."</p>
  <div className="mt-4 rounded-lg border-2 p-4" style={{borderColor: 'hsl(var(--stage-1) / 0.4)', backgroundColor: 'hsl(var(--stage-1) / 0.06)'}}>
    <p className="text-[10px] font-bold uppercase tracking-wider" style={{color: 'hsl(var(--stage-1))'}}>Example</p>
    <p className="mt-2 text-xs text-fd-foreground">"The buyer is the operations manager who will pay to reduce weekly reconciliation time because delays cost revenue."</p>
  </div>
</div>

If you cannot write this sentence, your buyer is still unclear.

## The First Decision
