## What a Truth Interview Is

A truth interview is a short, focused conversation designed to surface past behavior. It is not a sales call, a pitch test, or a product demo. The goal is not to be liked. The goal is to be clear.

<Callout type="warn" title="The core rule">
Past behavior beats future promises. "I would pay for that" is not evidence. "I already paid for something like that" is. You are not looking for approval. You are looking for proof.
</Callout>

## The 4-Step Interview Path

<div className="mt-4 flex flex-col gap-2">
  <div className="flex items-center gap-4 rounded-xl border-2 border-fd-border p-4">
    <span className="flex h-10 w-10 shrink-0 items-center justify-center rounded-full text-sm font-bold text-white" style={{backgroundColor: 'hsl(var(--stage-1))'}}>1</span>
    <div>
      <p className="text-sm font-semibold text-fd-foreground">Context</p>
      <p className="mt-0.5 text-xs text-fd-muted-foreground">"Walk me through a normal week." -- "Where does this problem show up?"</p>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div className="flex items-center gap-4 rounded-xl border-2 border-fd-border p-4">
    <span className="flex h-10 w-10 shrink-0 items-center justify-center rounded-full text-sm font-bold text-white" style={{backgroundColor: 'hsl(var(--stage-1))'}}>2</span>
    <div>
      <p className="text-sm font-semibold text-fd-foreground">Last Time</p>
      <p className="mt-0.5 text-xs text-fd-muted-foreground">"When was the last time this happened?" -- "What did you do next?"</p>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div className="flex items-center gap-4 rounded-xl border-2 border-fd-border p-4">
    <span className="flex h-10 w-10 shrink-0 items-center justify-center rounded-full text-sm font-bold text-white" style={{backgroundColor: 'hsl(var(--stage-1))'}}>3</span>
    <div>
      <p className="text-sm font-semibold text-fd-foreground">Tried or Paid</p>
      <p className="mt-0.5 text-xs text-fd-muted-foreground">"What have you already tried?" -- "Have you paid for anything to solve it?"</p>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div className="flex items-center gap-4 rounded-xl border-2 border-fd-border p-4">
    <span className="flex h-10 w-10 shrink-0 items-center justify-center rounded-full text-sm font-bold text-white" style={{backgroundColor: 'hsl(var(--stage-1))'}}>4</span>
    <div>
      <p className="text-sm font-semibold text-fd-foreground">Stakes</p>
      <p className="mt-0.5 text-xs text-fd-muted-foreground">"What did it cost you when it went wrong?" -- "What does a good outcome look like?"</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

This sequence forces real examples instead of hypothetical answers. Keep it to 15 minutes: 2 min context, 8 min core, 3 min clarify, 2 min thanks + referral.

After all interviews, look for repetition: same workaround across people, same cost across contexts, same trigger in different roles. Patterns are stronger evidence. Single stories are signals, not proof.
