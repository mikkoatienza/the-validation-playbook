## What Comes Next

You are about to move from "Is this pain real?" to "Is this pain worth it?" Stage 2 will ask a harder question. You will build a risk map, score the problem, and scan alternatives. That work only makes sense if your Stage 1 evidence is real.

If you pass this gate, you are no longer someone exploring ideas without evidence. You are a founder with evidence.
