## The Weekly Evidence Sprint

Every week has three moves. Keep them small and repeatable. Weekly is short enough to feel urgent and long enough to learn.

Amazon uses a structured weekly business review to focus on leading indicators. You do not need Amazon's scale. You need a weekly decision ritual that keeps you honest.

Without a log, you repeat the same mistakes. The log is not a journal. It is a record of tests, results, and decisions.

## Sprint Rules

<Callout type="warn" title="Keep the system honest">
- One test per week. More tests means less learning.
- One pass line. No pass line means no decision.
- One metric. More metrics means excuses.
- One change at a time. Two variables means you learn nothing.
</Callout>

If you miss a week, restart immediately. Do not stack two tests. One clean test beats two rushed ones.
