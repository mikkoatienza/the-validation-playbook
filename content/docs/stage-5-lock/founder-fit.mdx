<div className="mx-auto max-w-3xl">

You can have evidence and still fail if the wrong founder carries the problem. Founder fit is not about confidence. It is about whether you can win this fight with the assets you actually have.

<Callout type="idea" title="Output for this module">
- A founder-fit score (insight, access, credibility)
- A mitigation plan for weak areas
- A clear decision on whether to commit or adjust
</Callout>

You can be weak in one area if strong in the other two. If all three are weak, the fit is too fragile to commit. Quibi had Hollywood credibility but lacked insight into daily mobile behavior -- the right problem died in the wrong hands.

<Callout type="warn" title="Fit is not passion">
Passion helps you survive the work, but it does not earn trust. Fit is about the edge you bring. Write one sentence: "I can win this because ___." If you cannot write it, you do not yet have a clear edge.
</Callout>

<Callout type="error" title="Common traps">
- Confusing passion with insight
- Thinking a warm network equals access
- Assuming a title equals credibility
- Building for yourself but selling to someone else
- Ignoring weak fit because the idea is exciting
</Callout>
