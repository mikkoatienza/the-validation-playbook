## What Comes Next

You are about to move from "Do they come back?" to "Do we commit?" Stage 5 will ask you to decide whether to commit or stop, and whether you are the right founder for the fight. That work only makes sense if Stage 4 is real.

Resist the urge to scale. Stage 5 is where you decide, not where you accelerate.
