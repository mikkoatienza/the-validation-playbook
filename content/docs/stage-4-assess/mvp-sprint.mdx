You already have a problem worth solving, an offer, a price range, and a channel. Now you need proof that demand shows up when you put it in front of real people. This module is about testing, not building.

<Callout type="idea" title="Output for this module">
- An Experiment Card with a clear pass line
- A 7-day MVP test plan
- A pass/fail result with evidence notes
</Callout>

## MVP Means "Smallest Proof"

MVP usually stands for "minimum viable product." In this book, treat it as **minimum viable proof**. The goal is not a small product. The goal is a small test that proves demand exists.

## Pick a Test Type

Use the test that matches your riskiest question. Do not overbuild.

<div className="mt-4 grid gap-3 sm:grid-cols-2">
  <div className="rounded-xl border-2 border-fd-border p-4 transition-all hover:border-[hsl(var(--stage-4)/0.3)]">

  </div>

    <p className="mt-1.5 text-xs text-fd-muted-foreground">Small deposit for a limited version. Money is the strongest signal.</p>
    <p className="mt-1 text-[10px] italic" style={{color: 'hsl(var(--stage-4))'}}>Risk: willingness to pay</p>
  </div>
  <div className="rounded-xl border-2 border-fd-border p-4 transition-all hover:border-[hsl(var(--stage-4)/0.3)]">

    <p className="mt-1.5 text-xs text-fd-muted-foreground">15-min call ending with a paid next step. Tests urgency and budget fast.</p>
    <p className="mt-1 text-[10px] italic" style={{color: 'hsl(var(--stage-4))'}}>Risk: urgency</p>
  </div>

Pick one. A clean test beats a mixed test. If you are torn, choose the one that asks for money or time now.

## Set the Pass Line

<Callout type="warn" title="Set this before the test starts">
The pass line forces a decision. Without it, you will rationalize any result. Tie it to action, not views. Use a denominator. If you can move the line after the test, it was never a pass line.
</Callout>

<Callout type="error" title="Common traps">
- Building a full product before any proof
- Measuring clicks instead of commitments
- Changing the offer mid-test
- Asking for feedback instead of action
- Testing with fans who say yes to anything
- Setting a pass line after the results are in
</Callout>
