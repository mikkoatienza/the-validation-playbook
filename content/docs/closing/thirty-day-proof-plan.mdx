## Decision Rules

At the end of each week, choose one of three outcomes.

    <p className="text-sm font-semibold" style={{color: 'hsl(130, 60%, 45%)'}}>Advance</p>

The evidence is still thin. Tighten the test, do not stretch the timeline. If a week slips, tighten the test and move forward. The discipline is the proof.

## If You Finish Early

Do not skip weeks. Use the extra time to:

- Retest the strongest signal with a tighter pass line
- Expand to one adjacent segment and compare results
- Strengthen the evidence log and decision note

Fast decisions are good. Repeated decisions are better.

## If You Go Beyond 30 Days

If you decide to continue, keep the weekly cadence. That is the real habit. If you decide to stop, keep the evidence. It will save you on the next idea.
