<Callout type="idea" title="The core problem this playbook solves">
Most founders do not fail from lack of effort. They fail from the wrong kind of effort: building before proof. This playbook replaces guesswork with evidence so you build the right idea -- not just the exciting one.
</Callout>

The Validation Playbook is a story-driven guide for early-stage founders who want conviction without hype. It teaches a practical method for finding real pain, testing the riskiest assumptions first, and earning commitment before building anything heavy.

This book does not promise guaranteed outcomes. It gives you a repeatable way to increase your odds and build around proof instead of hope.

After the five stages, two extensions cover Positioning and Edge and Momentum Loops. The playbook ends with a 30-Day Proof Plan you can run immediately.

## How Each Stage Works

<Callout type="info" title="The cadence">
Every stage follows the same rhythm: read the stage opening, complete three modules with specific exercises, review your evidence, and decide whether to advance, tighten, or reset.
</Callout>

Each stage uses a different format to keep the learning fresh -- workshop, myth-bust, story-to-framework, field manual, and letter. You are not asked to do everything at once. You are asked to earn the next stage before you move forward.
